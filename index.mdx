## Who is InsureStack for?

InsureStack is built for:

- **Insurance brokers** seeking to automate repetitive tasks like quote requests and policy analysis
- **Brokerage teams** looking to improve efficiency and accuracy across quoting, submissions, and commission tracking
- **Operations managers** wanting visibility into team activities, credit usage, and team performance
- **Agencies** needing to scale operations without proportional headcount increases

### Common Use Cases

<Accordion title="Quote Generation">
  Request quotes from 10+ carriers simultaneously for commercial auto, trucking, and general liability risks - reducing manual quoting times from hours to minutes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Policy Comparison">
  Analyze coverage differences between incumbent and renewal policies to identify gaps and competitive advantages.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Commission Tracking">
  Extract commission data from carrier statements to reconcile income, track trends, and identify your most profitable carriers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Proposal Generation">
  Create professional proposal packages from policies of your choice using custom templates based on your styling.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Submission Generation">
  Automatically generate complete and accurate ACORD forms and submission packages, reducing errors and missing information that lead to carrier rejections.
</Accordion>
