## What is Quoting?

The **Quoting** module is the industry's most comprehensive commercial lines comparative rater. Upload your documents and get quotes across multiple carriers in minutes with zero manual data entry - InsureStack handles the entire workflow automatically.

## Supported Lines of Business

InsureStack integrates with 30+ carriers across:

- **Commercial Auto** - Business vehicle coverage
- **BOP** (Business Owners' Policy) - Combined property and liability
- **WC** (Workers' Compensation) - Employee injury coverage
- **GL** (General Liability) - Business liability protection
- **Trucking** - Commercial trucking insurance
- **Professional Liability** - E&O and professional coverage

<Tip>
  Enterprise clients can request additional carriers to be prioritized for integration.
</Tip>

## How to Generate Quotes

<Steps>
<Step title="Upload Any Document">

1. Navigate to **Quoting** from the sidebar
2. Select **LOB** (Line of Business):
   - Commercial Auto
   - BOP (Business Owners' Policy)
   - WC (Workers' Compensation)
   - GL (General Liability)
   - Trucking
   - Professional Liability

3. Select **Insurance Carrier** for this LOB
4. **Upload documents** - ACORDs, intake forms, supplementals, or previous policies
5. **Provide Customer Name**
6. Click **"Parse Documents"** and wait for AI to extract data
7. **Verify** how the information was mapped to carrier form fields
8. Click **"Start Quote"**

<Note>
  AI automatically scans your documents and extracts information, mapping it to carrier form fields. Review this mapping to ensure accuracy before starting the quote.
</Note>

</Step>

<Step title="AI Scans & Quotes">

InsureStack automatically processes your quote:

- **Created** - Quote request has been created
- **Queued** - Waiting to start processing
- **Running** - AI is filling out carrier forms automatically
- **Completed** - Quote successfully generated

<Tip>
  You can navigate away while quotes generate in parallel. Check the Dashboard's Recent Activity to monitor progress.
</Tip>

</Step>

<Step title="Review & Bind">

Once completed, review quotes in the carrier portal:

- View **premium amounts** and coverage details
- **Download quote PDFs** directly
- **Bind and pay** through the carrier portal
- **Compare quotes** across multiple carriers

</Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Quote Statuses

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="spinner" title="Running">
    AI is automatically filling out the carrier form. This typically takes 3-10 minutes depending on form complexity.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="check" title="Completed">
    Quote successfully generated. Review premium, coverage details, and binding options in the carrier portal. You can download the quote PDF.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="ban" title="Declined">
    Carrier declined to quote based on risk appetite or underwriting guidelines. Check the decline reason and try a different carrier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="xmark" title="Failed">
    Technical error occurred during quote generation. This could be due to carrier website changes, missing required information, or connectivity issues. Contact support if failures persist.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Viewing Your Quotes

The quote list displays all your quote requests with key details:

### Quote Card Information

Each quote shows:

- **Customer name** - Who the quote is for
- **Carrier** - Which insurance company
- **Premium** - Quote amount (displayed when completed)
- **Status** - Current state (Running, Completed, Declined, Failed)
- **Created date** - When the quote was requested
- **LOB** - Line of business

## Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Quote declined - what does this mean?">
    The carrier has declined to quote based on their underwriting guidelines. Common reasons:
    - Risk outside carrier's appetite
    - Missing required information
    - Loss history exceeds thresholds
    - Business type not covered

    **Solution:** Review the decline reason, address any issues, and try a different carrier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Quote failed - what should I do?">
    Technical errors can occur due to:
    - Carrier website changes
    - Missing critical form fields
    - Connectivity issues

    **Solution:**
    1. Review your uploaded documents for completeness
    2. Retry the quote
    3. If failures persist, contact support with the quote ID
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Why is my quote taking so long?">
    Most quotes complete in 5-15 minutes. Longer processing times may indicate:
    - Complex risk requiring extensive form filling
    - Carrier website slowness
    - Additional information required

    **Solution:** Check the Recent Activity on your Dashboard. If status remains "Running" for 30+ minutes, contact support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Credit Usage

Quote generation consumes credits based on:
- Carrier selected
- Line of business
- Form complexity

<Note>
  Credits are only consumed when the quote reaches a final state (completed, failed, or declined). Quotes that are terminated mid-process do not consume credits.
</Note>

Learn more about [credit costs](/billing/overview) and [subscription plans](/billing/plans).

## What Happens After Quoting?

Once you have quotes:

1. **Review in carrier portal** - View premiums, coverage, and terms across all quotes
2. **Bind and pay directly** - Complete binding and payment through the carrier portal
3. **Upload bound policy** - After binding, upload the policy to InsureStack for analysis
4. **Generate proposals** - Use the [Policy Comparison](/features/policies-advanced) tools to create client proposals
