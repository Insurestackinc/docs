## Available Plans

InsureStack offers tiered plans with different monthly credit allocations. Choose the plan that matches your usage needs.

<Note>
  All plans include **10 free monthly credits**. Paid plans add additional credits on top.
</Note>

## Plan Comparison

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Price | Team Members | Best For |
|------|----------------|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Pay as you Go** | 10 free | **Free** | 1 | Testing the platform, very light usage |
| **Plus** | 40 total (10 free + 30 paid) | **$20/month** | 1 | Small agencies, individual brokers |
| **Pro** | 360 total (10 free + 350 paid) | **$200/month** | 1 | Growing brokerages, moderate volume |
| **Team** | 2,010 total (10 free + 2,000 paid) | **$999/month** | Multiple | Multi-user teams, high volume |
| **Enterprise** | Custom Package | **Custom Pricing** | Multiple | Large organizations, enterprise needs |

## Pay as you Go (Free)

### What You Get

- **10 free monthly credits**
- Access to all core features
- Single user
- Community support

### Who It's For

- **Testing the platform** - Try InsureStack before committing
- **Very light usage** - Occasional quote or policy analysis
- **Individual brokers** - Solo practitioners with minimal automation needs

## Plus Plan - $20/month

### What You Get

- **40 monthly credits** (10 free + 30 paid)
- All core features
- Single user
- Email support

### Who It's For

- **Small agencies** - 1-2 person operations
- **Individual brokers** - Independent agents with regular quoting needs
- **Limited volume** - 30-40 actions per month
- **Getting started** - Growing your automation gradually

## Pro Plan - $200/month

### What You Get

- **360 monthly credits** (10 free + 350 paid)
- All core features
- Single user
- Priority email support
- Advanced policy tools

### Who It's For

- **Growing brokerages** - Scaling operations
- **Moderate volume** - 300+ actions per month
- **Heavy quoting** - Regularly comparing multiple carriers
- **Serious automation** - Committed to digitizing workflows

## Team Plan - $999/month

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

    Learn more about [plan changes](/billing/plan-changes).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="What if I run out of credits mid-month?">
    You can purchase additional credits ($1 each) that work with any plan. Alternatively, you can upgrade to a higher plan for more monthly credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Do unused credits roll over?">
    Monthly credits (free + plan) do NOT roll over - they expire at the end of each billing period. Only purchased credits roll over (valid for 1 year).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. ACH and wire transfers are available for Enterprise plans only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
