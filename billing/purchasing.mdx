## Option 1: One-Time Credit Purchase

Purchase individual credits at **$1 per credit** that work with any plan.

### Benefits

- **Valid for 1 year** from purchase date
- **Roll over month-to-month** until expiration
- **Work with any plan** - even if you downgrade or cancel
- **No commitment** - Buy only what you need
- **Stack with monthly credits** - Add to your existing allocation

### When to Buy Credits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="calendar-days" title="Occasional high-usage months">
    You normally use 30 credits/month but have a busy quarter coming up with 60 credits needed. Buy 30 extra credits instead of upgrading your plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Safety buffer">
    Buy extra credits as insurance to ensure you never run out mid-workflow, even during unexpected busy periods.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="piggy-bank" title="Avoid upgrading temporarily">
    If you only occasionally need more than your plan provides, buying credits is more cost-effective than upgrading for just one or two months.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Can I buy credits and upgrade at the same time?">
    Yes! Purchased credits stack with your plan's monthly allocation. Many customers buy a safety buffer of purchased credits even when on a paid plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="What happens to purchased credits if I upgrade?">
    They remain available and continue to be valid for 1 year from purchase. They stack with your new plan's credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Can I get a refund on purchased credits?">
    Purchased credits are non-refundable once added to your account. However, they're valid for 1 year, so you have plenty of time to use them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Do purchased credits expire if I cancel my subscription?">
    No. Purchased credits remain valid for 1 year from purchase, even if you cancel your subscription and return to the Pay as you Go plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Is there a minimum or maximum purchase amount?">
    Minimum: 1 credit ($1). There is no maximum - buy as many as you need.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="circle-question" title="Can I buy credits for someone else's organization?">
    No. Credits are tied to the organization and can only be purchased by someone with billing access in that organization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
