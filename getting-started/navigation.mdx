## Sidebar Navigation

The platform uses a **collapsible vertical sidebar** on the left side as your primary navigation tool.

## Sidebar Structure

The sidebar is organized into three main sections:

### Top Section
- **InsureStack logo** - Click to return to dashboard
- **Chevron icon** - Collapse sidebar to get more space for your workspace 
- **Organization name** - Displays your company/org name

### Additional Controls

At the very bottom of the sidebar:

- **Theme toggle** - Switch between Light, Dark, and System modes
- **User menu** - Shows your email and logout option


## Navigation Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Quick Action Cards">
    Use the Dashboard's Quick Action Cards to jump directly into workflows without navigating through the sidebar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="arrow-left" title="Back Navigation">
    Most pages include breadcrumb navigation or back buttons to help you retrace your steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="bookmark" title="Bookmarking Pages">
    You can bookmark frequently used pages in your browser for quick access (e.g., bookmark the Quoting page).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="Mobile Experience">
    As of now, InsureStack is optimized for desktop browsers and we recommend using a desktop for the best experience. Mobile support is functional but limited and is something that we are working on currently.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
